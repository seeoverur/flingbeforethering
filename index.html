The Best Charleston Bachelorette Activity List

Finding the Perfect Charleston Bachelorette Activity

Choosing the right Charleston bachelorette activity is essential when visiting the Holy City. This coastal destination becomes a prime location for girls' trips specifically from March through October. During these months, the weather is warm enough for beach days but the city breeze keeps evening strolls comfortable, making it the peak season for groups looking to celebrate before the wedding.

1

Candle Making at Candlefish

For a creative and scented group experience, Candlefish on King Street offers private candle-making workshops. This is a favorite Charleston bachelorette activity because it allows the group to BYOB while exploring a library of over 100 scents. Each person gets to pour their own custom candles to take home as a memento of the trip.

2

Ghost and Haunted Tours with Old South Carriage

Charleston is known for its history and haunts. Booking a carriage tour with Old South Carriage Company offers a spooky yet historical way to see the city at night. It is a relaxed Charleston bachelorette activity that combines sightseeing with chilling tales of the city's past, perfect for groups who want entertainment without walking long distances.

3

Entertainer - Michael At Your Place

For a private and high-energy experience, Michael At Your Place provides professional entertainment that comes directly to your rental or venue. Booking Michael After Dark is a great option for groups wanting a personalized show in the comfort of their own space. It stands out as a memorable Charleston bachelorette activity that brings the excitement to you rather than requiring a trip to a crowded club.

4

Beach Day: Isle of Palms or Folly Beach

No trip to the Lowcountry is complete without time by the ocean. Spending a day at Isle of Palms offers a more upscale, resort-style vibe, while Folly Beach provides a funky, laid-back atmosphere. A beach day is the quintessential daytime Charleston bachelorette activity for relaxing in the sun before a night out downtown.

5

Rainbow Row Photo Ops

The cluster of 13 pastel-colored historic houses on East Bay Street is an iconic landmark. A walk to Rainbow Row serves as an excellent low-cost Charleston bachelorette activity. It provides the perfect backdrop for group photos and captures the historic charm that defines the downtown peninsula.

6

Spa Day at The Dewberry

For ultimate relaxation, The Dewberry Spa offers a luxury escape with treatments inspired by local botanicals. Located in a mid-century modern hotel, this spa experience is a sophisticated Charleston bachelorette activity for groups looking to unwind and pamper the bride before the big day.

7

Private Chef Experience

Hiring a private chef to cook a multi-course meal at your rental property is becoming increasingly popular. This Charleston bachelorette activity allows the group to enjoy high-quality local cuisine without the stress of making large dinner reservations. It creates an intimate setting for toasts and games while enjoying Lowcountry flavors.
